# Financial Psychology Course: Curriculum Design & Structure
## Breaking the Money Taboo: An Integrated Psychological Approach

### COURSE OVERVIEW

**Title**: "Money Mindset Mastery: Integrating Psychology and Personal Finance"

**Tagline**: "Heal your relationship with money through evidence-based psychological approaches"

**Duration**: 12 weeks (or modular self-paced)

**Target Audience**:
- Individuals struggling with financial anxiety, avoidance, or compulsive behaviors
- Couples experiencing money conflicts
- People wanting to understand emotional drivers of financial decisions
- Financial advisers seeking to integrate psychology into practice
- Therapists wanting to address clients' money issues

**Learning Outcomes**:
By the end of this course, participants will:
1. Understand the psychological roots of their financial behaviors
2. Identify and challenge money-related cognitive distortions
3. Align financial actions with core values
4. Recognize and work with different "money parts"
5. Understand how attachment style affects financial relationships
6. Identify and begin healing early maladaptive schemas about money
7. Build a personalized financial psychology practice
8. Reduce financial shame and anxiety
9. Improve financial communication in relationships
10. Create sustainable behavioral change

## COURSE DELIVERY FORMATS

### Format Option 1: Live Cohort (12 weeks)

**Structure**:
- Weekly content release (Mondays)
- Self-paced video/reading during week
- Live workshop each week (90 min, same day/time)
- Forum discussions ongoing
- Small group accountability meetings (optional)

**Pros**:
- Community cohesion
- Live interaction with instructor
- Peer support and accountability
- Shared timeline creates momentum

**Cons**:
- Requires time commitment at specific times
- May not work for all schedules
- Can't go faster or slower than cohort

**Recommended for**: People who value live interaction and community support

---

### Format Option 2: Self-Paced with Support (6-24 weeks)

**Structure**:
- All content available immediately
- Complete at own pace (minimum 6 weeks, maximum 6 months)
- Monthly live Q&A sessions (attend any)
- Asynchronous forum
- Option to join any live cohort workshops
- Email support

**Pros**:
- Maximum flexibility
- Can review content multiple times
- Work around schedule
